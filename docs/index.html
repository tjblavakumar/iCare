Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

iCare ("we", "our", or "the app") is a wellness check-in app that helps you stay connected with loved ones through simple emoji status updates. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for account creation, login, and password recovery
  • Phone number (optional) - Alternative login method
  • Display name - Shown to your connections

Usage Data

  • Status updates - Emoji and mood labels you share with your circle
  • Connections - List of people you've connected with
  • Notifications - Records of alerts sent and received

Device Information

  • FCM Token - Used solely for delivering push notifications

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication - To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Core functionality - To share your status with your circle and show you their status
  • Notifications - To alert you when someone in your circle needs support
  • Account recovery - To help you regain access if you forget your password

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication - Secure login credentials
  • Cloud Firestore - User profiles, connections, and status updates
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging - Push notification delivery

Data is stored in the United States and protected by Firebase's security measures.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:
  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Share your information with third parties (except as required by law)

We DO share:

  • Your status updates with people YOU have approved as connections
  • Your display name with users searching for you (to enable connections)

Your Rights

You can:

  • View your data - See all your information in the app's Settings
  • Update your data - Change your display name and status anytime
  • Delete your account - Permanently remove all your data from Settings → Delete Account
When you delete your account:
  • All your personal data is permanently erased
  • You are removed from all connections
  • Your circle members are notified that you've left

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted immediately
  • Status history: Last 30 days retained for your connections to view

Children's Privacy

iCare is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Email verification for new accounts
  • No plain-text password storage

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
